Privacy Policy

Privacy matters to us.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard it when you use Go Ride.

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains the information we collect, how we process it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

  • Ride booking data
  • Account information
  • Security and support data
Introduction

We respect your privacy and keep our approach simple

At Go Ride, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We aim to provide clear, practical information about your data and your choices without overwhelming you with legal language.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide a reliable experience

Personal details

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Trip and usage information

  • Pickup and destination details
  • Ride history
  • Location information when booking or tracking rides

Technical information

  • Device and browser details
  • IP address and app usage data
  • Support and communication records

Payments

  • Payment details where applicable
  • Billing and transaction records
How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide, improve, and protect the service

To provide rides

We use your information to facilitate bookings, connect riders and drivers, and support trip completion.

To improve services

We may use usage data to understand feedback, improve performance, and enhance the user experience.

To support customers

We use contact details to respond to support requests, address concerns, and confirm important updates.

For safety and fraud prevention

We use information to reduce misuse, protect users, and maintain safety standards across the platform.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures such as secure storage practices, restricted access, and encrypted transmission where applicable. We do our best to protect your information, although no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

We use cookies to support functionality and improve the experience

We may use essential cookies to keep the site functioning properly, analytics cookies to understand traffic and performance, and preference cookies to remember selected settings. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, though some parts of the service may not function as intended if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

We may rely on trusted third parties to support our services

We may use third-party providers for payment processing, mapping, analytics, or support tools. These providers may process some information on our behalf in line with their own privacy practices, and we take reasonable steps to ensure they handle data responsibly.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and support ongoing service needs.

Your Rights

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion

You may request access to your personal information, ask for corrections, or request deletion where legally permitted. If you want to exercise any of these rights, please contact our support team using the details below.

Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children below the applicable age

Our service is not intended for children below the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction. Where required by local law, parental consent may be needed before using the service.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please check this page periodically for the latest version.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy?

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our Contact page or email support@goride.com.
